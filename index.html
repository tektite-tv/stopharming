This website is not a plea, it’s a patchwork recollection.
A scattered archive of my lived experience as someone
failed again and again by Ontario’s care systems.
What you see here is the human cost of indifference.
This isn’t about closure—it’s about exposure.
My name is Thomas Harrison. I live with neurodivergent disability,
and for years, I have not received the care I need to thrive.
This website documents my journey through a system that
dehumanizes, deflects, and discards. Since at least May 2025,
I’ve been sounding the alarm—ignored, punished, and left behind.
These entries, images, and statements are fragments of truth
pieced together to reclaim my voice in a province that tried
to erase me.
